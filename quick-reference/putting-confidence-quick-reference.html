Pre-Round Checklist & On-Course Keys
GolfGenii
Before You Tee Off
Green Speed CheckHit 5 lag putts from 30 feet. Feel the pace.
Short Putt Warm-UpMake 10 consecutive 3-footers before playing.
Routine Run-ThroughWalk through your full routine on 3 putts. No score.
Confirm Your GripLight pressure. No tension in forearms or shoulders.
Set Your Intention"I am a confident putter and I trust my read."
On Every Green
Walk to Green, Read SlopeOverall slope reading starts from 20 yards out.
Low-Side ReadAlways crouch from the low side to see true break.
Pick Your ApexAim at the high point, not the hole.
Full Routine. Every Time.Never step in without completing your routine.
Head Still After ImpactListen for the ball to drop — don't watch.
Reset ImmediatelyMiss? One breath, walk to next hole with a clean slate.
If It Falls Apart
Missing Left ConsistentlyCheck face alignment — it's likely open. Slow down setup.
Leaving Putts ShortYour follow-through is decelerating. Accelerate through impact.
3-PuttingSpeed problem, not direction. Return to Ladder Drill.
Nervous on Short PuttsSlow your routine down by 50%. Breathe before triggering.
RushingStop. Go back to step 1 of your routine. Start over.
